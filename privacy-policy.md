# Privacy Policy for NumBee Learning

**Last Updated: November 2, 2025**

## Our Commitment to Privacy

NumBee Learning ("we", "our", or "the App") is committed to protecting the privacy of children and their families. This privacy policy explains our data practices.

---

## Information We Do NOT Collect

NumBee Learning **does NOT collect, store, or transmit any personal information**, including:

- No names or personal identifiers
- No email addresses
- No location data
- No device identifiers (IDFA, IDFV)
- No usage analytics
- No crash reports containing personal data
- No IP addresses
- No browsing history
- No search history

**Zero data collection. Period.**

---

## Local Data Storage

All app data is stored **locally on your device only**:

### What is Stored Locally:
- Learning progress and scores
- Activity completion history
- Earned stickers and achievements
- App settings and preferences (language, font, number range)
- Premium unlock status

### Important:
- Data **never leaves your device**
- Data is **NOT backed up to our servers**
- Data is **NOT transmitted over the internet**
- Data can be **deleted by uninstalling the app**
- We have **NO access** to any data on your device

---

## Speech Recognition

### How Speech Practice Works:

**For the Speech Practice activity (premium feature):**
- Voice audio is processed **on-device only** using Apple's Speech Recognition framework
- Audio is **never recorded, stored, or transmitted**
- Processing happens in real-time and is immediately discarded
- No audio data leaves your device
- No audio is accessible to us

**Microphone permission is requested only when:**
- User taps on Speech Practice activity
- Can be denied - all other activities work without microphone

---

## Third-Party Services

NumBee Learning **does NOT use**:

- Analytics services (Google Analytics, Firebase, etc.)
- Advertising networks
- Crash reporting services
- Social media integrations
- Cloud storage services
- Third-party tracking tools
- Any third-party SDKs that collect data

**The app works completely offline after download.**

## Children's Privacy (COPPA Compliance)

NumBee Learning is designed for children ages 3-8 and **fully complies** with the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act (COPPA):

### Our Commitments:
- We do **NOT knowingly collect** personal information from children
- We do **NOT share** any information with third parties
- No advertising or external links within the app
- No social features or user-generated content
- No communication between users
- The app works **completely offline**
- No parental consent needed (because we collect nothing)

### Safe Environment:
- No chat or messaging features
- No user profiles or accounts
- No ability to share information
- No external web browsing
- No in-app purchases visible to children (locked in Settings)

---

## In-App Purchases

### Premium Unlock:
- Optional one-time purchase ($6.99)
- Handled through **Apple's secure In-App Purchase system**
- We do **NOT process** or **store** any payment information
- All transactions managed by Apple
- **Family Sharing enabled** - one purchase covers up to 6 family members

### What We Know About Purchases:
- Only whether premium is unlocked (stored locally on device)
- No billing information
- No purchase history
- No personal details
